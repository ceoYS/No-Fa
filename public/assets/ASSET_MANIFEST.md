## Important notes

- `white_kitten_blink.webp` is not a dedicated blink-only image; it uses the closest existing closed-eye white kitten frame.

- Item assets are derived crops from the recovered room image. They are not transparent standalone production assets. Use them as temporary placeholders only until proper item renders are supplied.

- `ember_room_empty.webp` means “no cat in the room,” not an unfurnished empty room. It contains furniture and care objects.

- Extra colored cat assets are included: `black_guardian.webp`, `gray_mentor.webp`, `brown_loaf.webp`, `family_four_cats.webp`, `state_sheet_cats.webp`.


## MISSING
